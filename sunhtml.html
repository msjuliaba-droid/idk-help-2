Friendship, a thing that people from all over the world get to experience, or so they think. The definition “A relationship of mutual affection between people” (Wikipedia 2019) simply waters down how deep and connecting a bond like this can be. Every individual gets a taste of what friendship feels like at some point in their life, however, very few meet their soul person. On Maslow’s hierarchy of needs, love and belonging is ranked third, while in my opinion, if you meet the right person, you will see that it should be as low as physiological needs. I will attempt to demonstrate that friendship is vital, and that ours is one of a kind. It seems as if no one else has met their best friend, and on the occasion that they have, it may seem plain or even shallow. When speaking with friends or individuals I am surrounded with, I constantly hear that they have not found their person. They may have formed deep bonds with others, but they find it difficult to name someone as their best friend. In the case that someone can, and I hear them speak about their friend, it sounds like friendship, but far from what we have. While for others it may seem like chance that they met each other, for us it feels like destiny. After 8 years together, it feels like our minds have interwoven. Saying the same thing at the same time (ahlelele ahlela 2025?), having the same viewpoints, keeping no secrets from each other or simply being ourselves, has let us achieve what I believe to be the highest level of friendship. Another argument that backs this up is our own parents claiming that our bond is special. Even years ago, my mother would tell me that she believed we were twins in a past life and somehow reunited. We can see, that not only we, but also other people claim that our closeness is intended. We have little to no secrets kept from each other. Sometimes I ponder is there is something I have not told you, and I simply cannot think of anything. If anything happens, whether it is positive, negative or boring, you are the first person I run to tell. We manage to trust each other in vulnerable states, our trauma dumps or just wanting comfort from one another during a moment of stress. It is rare that we are not ashamed to tell someone something that may cause them to judge us, because we know we would not do that to each other. While these are only two of many reasons that demonstrate our relationship, I think it is enough to prove that we are one in a million. A definition does not compare to what we have and what we have found in each other. (I would write more but like I got other shit to write and do broski)
